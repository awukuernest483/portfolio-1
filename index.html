I'm a Fashion Designer

I craft garments that inspire confidence and celebrate individuality.

I am a passionate and innovative fashion designer with a strong foundation in fashion design and textiles. My journey in fashion is driven by creativity, technical expertise, and a deep understanding of industry trends. I specialize in designing custom garments that are both stylish and functional, with a focus on pattern making, garment construction, and fabric selection. With experience in both freelance work and internships, I have honed my ability to transform concepts into beautifully crafted pieces. I am committed to pushing the boundaries of fashion and delivering exceptional results in every project I undertake. Whether working on a new collection or custom designs, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical skill to the table, always aiming to create garments that empower and inspire.
